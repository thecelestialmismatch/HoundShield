Common questions

Before you sign up.

Does the free tier scan my actual prompts?

Yes. All 16 detection engines run on Free — the only difference is daily request volume (500/day) and log retention (7 days). Your CUI never leaves your network on any tier.

How is my data not seen by HoundShield servers?

Scanning runs locally in the proxy process you deploy. Only metadata (license key hash, prompt count, timestamp) reaches our control plane. Prompt content stays inside your perimeter.

Will my C3PAO accept the PDF report?

Yes — the PDF cites NIST SP 800-171 Rev 2 controls by number and includes the SHA-256 hash-chained violation log. We built it from feedback with three C3PAOs.

Can I deploy on-prem / air-gapped?

Enterprise and Agency tiers ship a Docker image you run inside your own perimeter. Air-gapped mode available — no outbound connections at all.

What if I exceed my plan?

We don't hard-block — we email you at 80% and 100% and let you upgrade in-app. No surprise overage charges.
